Our Mission

The Aircon era is over.

Contributing to Net Zero goals by creating a solar-powered climate cooling system that uses ZERO aircon and can be fitted into dongas, freezers and other fixed-asset buildings.

Reduce Co2 Emissions

Powered by renewables. No use of Aircon. Low air distribution consumption (low kW/RT) with an operating cost of $0.08 per kW. A true Net Zero solution.

Reduce Non-Renewables

No more depence on diesel generators to cool worker buildings and accommodations. 1/3 of harvest solar power cools buildings for 24 hours. The other 2/3 can be deployed for other purposes.

Improve WHS parameters

Optimal temp/RH maintained 24/7, automatically adjusting to occupancy levels. Our climate cooling system produces no additional Co2. Automatic humidity control deters mould.

Sustainable

No Co2 emissions as renewables are used. Our climate cooling system is recyclable, non-toxic and safe in terms of flammability.

Project Scope

Deploy our dongas equipped with solar-powered climate cooling system across multiple latitudes of Western Australia to prove efficacy and durability.
