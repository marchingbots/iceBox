Our Mission

To substantively contribute to Net-Zero goals via solar-powered climate control systems fitted into dongas, freezers and other fixed-asset buildings including warehouses and offices.


Pilot Project Scope


The Modern Donga.

Off-grid.
Solar-powered.
Stable interior temperatures.
Steady 60% RH.
No mould.
No Co2 accumulation.
Grid backup capable.


Deploy shipping containers outfitted with solar-powered phase change material systems across multiple latitudes in Western Australia to demonstrate efficacy and durability.

Proven Deliverables:

Energy Efficient:

Low operating cost: $0.08 per kW to operate.

Lower air distribution consumption - Low kW/RT

Net positive and efficient solution using 1/3 of the solar power generated to cool for 24 hours. 2/3 can be deployed for other uses.

Environmental Sustainability:

Net Zero Solution - no Co2 emissions as renewables are used.

Phase Change Material is recyclable, non-toxic and safe in terms of flammability.

Improved Indoor Air Quality:

No additional Co2 produced.

Optimal temp/RH maintained 24/7 automatically adjusting to occupancy levels.

Automatic humidity control deters black mould.
